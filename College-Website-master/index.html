Courses We Offer

ISMT British College offers a range of innovative programs designed to equip students with cutting-edge skills and knowledge in technology, management, and beyond.

BHM

ISMT College’s Bachelor of Hotel Management (BHM) course, detailed on ismt.edu.np, offers immersive education in hospitality, culinary arts, and tourism management. This program blends theory and practical training to build leadership, communication, and management skills. Students gain global industry insights and confidence. Enhance your professional journey. Accelerate your promising future.

BSc(Hons).IT

ISMT College’s BSc (Hons.) IT program offers a comprehensive curriculum blending theoretical knowledge with practical skills in information technology. Students gain expertise in software development, networking, data management, and emerging technologies. With industry-oriented projects and experienced faculty, the course prepares graduates for careers in IT, fostering innovation and professional growth.

BBA

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) program at ISMT College (ismt.edu.np) is designed to provide students with a robust foundation in the world of business and management. This comprehensive program equips aspiring professionals with the theoretical knowledge and practical skills necessary to excel in today's competitive and dynamic business environment.

About Us

Established in 2011, the International School of Management and Technology (ISMT) College in Kathmandu offers internationally recognized British degrees in collaboration with the University of Sunderland and Pearson BTEC. ISMT provides programs such as BSc (Hons) Computer Systems Engineering, BA (Hons) Business and Management, and BSc (Hons) International Tourism and Hospitality Management. The college emphasizes practical learning through project work, discussions, and presentations, supported by modern facilities including air-conditioned classrooms, well-equipped IT labs, and a comprehensive library. ISMT also offers credit transfer opportunities to over 150 universities worldwide, enabling students to complete their degrees abroad.
